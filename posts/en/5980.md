On Saturday, we decided to run a mini Maker Carnival on the container hackerspace currently in front of Grand Gateway in Xujiahui, Shanghai. This being one of the busiest intersections in Shanghai, we attracted a lot of interested people and met some interesting people. Thanks to the volunteers from our space, Make+ and DFRobot.
